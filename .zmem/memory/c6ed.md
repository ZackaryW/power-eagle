# Power Eagle serves Eagle users and community script authors who need fast discovery, installation, and sharing of lightweight automation plugins.

## Context
- The product targets users who find Eagle's native extension workflow too slow or heavy for small utilities.
- It also targets script authors who want to publish and iterate on small tools without shipping full extensions.

## Content
- Users discover plugin URLs through GitHub, forums, social posts, or direct sharing.
- Installation is URL-first: paste a zip URL, download it, validate it, and activate it quickly.
- Management should stay simple: enable, disable, remove, and re-install to update.
- The experience is optimized for quick utilities, automation, and community sharing rather than large standalone apps.

## Consequences / notes
- UX should minimize installation friction and keep management understandable to non-extension developers.
- Success is measured by reliable installs, easy updates, strong isolation, and practical plugin capabilities across JavaScript and Python.
